About MindEase

MindEase was founded by Krisztian Kasos, PhD, psychologist and researcher.

MindEase was created to offer accessible, practical, and evidence-informed support for people experiencing stress, anxiety, and emotional overload in everyday life.

Krisztian’s background includes psychology, behavioural science, emotional regulation, psychophysiology, guided relaxation, mindfulness-based methods, and cognitive-behavioural approaches.

The aim of MindEase is to combine scientific knowledge with practical tools that people can actually use — whether through guided audio practices, educational materials, workshops, or individual support.
