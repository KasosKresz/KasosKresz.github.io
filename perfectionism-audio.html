Perfectionism Audio Series

Perfectionism can create ongoing pressure, self-criticism, and fear of getting things wrong. This series is designed to help you soften that pressure and build a more flexible, compassionate way of relating to yourself.

Listen to the Series

Part I: Escaping Perfectionism

An introduction to stepping out of rigid standards and constant self-pressure.

Part II: Embracing Imperfection

A guided practice for accepting imperfection with more kindness and realism.

Part III: Letting Go of Others' Approval

A reflection on easing the need to be constantly validated or seen as flawless.

Part IV: The Journey of Embracing Imperfection

A longer practice focused on building a more flexible and sustainable inner mindset.

Part V: Embracing Mistakes

A guided exercise for relating to mistakes with less fear and more self-compassion.
