About MindEase

MindEase was founded by Krisztian Kasos, PhD, psychologist and researcher.

MindEase was created to offer accessible, practical, and evidence-informed support for people experiencing stress, anxiety, and emotional overload in everyday life.

Krisztian’s background includes psychology, behavioural science, emotional regulation, guided relaxation, mindfulness-based approaches, psychophysiology, and cognitive-behavioural methods.

The aim of MindEase is to bring together scientific understanding and practical tools that people can actually use — through workshops, individual consultation, guided audio, blog content, and supporting materials.
