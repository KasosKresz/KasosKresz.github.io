Articles and reflections on stress, anxiety, relaxation, and emotional wellbeing.

Understanding Stress

Stress is a natural response, but when it becomes chronic it can affect both mind and body. Learning to notice the early signs can help people respond more effectively.

How Breathing Can Help Anxiety

Slow and steady breathing can reduce physiological arousal and help calm the nervous system during anxious moments.

Why Relaxation Practice Matters

Relaxation is not only helpful during acute stress. Practised regularly, it can improve emotional balance and build resilience over time.
