Stress & Anxiety Management Workshops

Practical, supportive workshops to help you better understand stress and anxiety, build emotional resilience, and learn simple techniques for everyday wellbeing.

About the Workshops

These workshops are designed for people who would like to better understand how stress and anxiety affect the mind and body, and how to respond to them in a healthier way.

The sessions focus on practical, evidence-informed methods that participants can use in daily life to feel more grounded, more aware, and better able to cope with difficult moments.

What You Will Learn

During the workshops, participants will be introduced to key ideas about stress and anxiety, including physical symptoms, emotional signs, thought patterns, and behavioural responses.

We will also explore helpful tools such as breathing exercises, relaxation methods, mindfulness-based practices, and practical coping strategies that can support long-term wellbeing.

Workshop Topics

Understanding stress and anxiety

Recognising common physical, emotional, and cognitive signs

Breathing and relaxation exercises

Mindfulness and grounding techniques

Developing healthier coping strategies
