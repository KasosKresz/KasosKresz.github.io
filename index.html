Stress & Anxiety Management Workshops

Supportive, practical workshops to help you better understand stress and anxiety, learn effective calming techniques, and build healthier ways of coping in everyday life.

About the Workshops

These workshops are designed for people who would like to better understand how stress and anxiety affect the mind and body, and how to respond to them in a healthier and more balanced way.

The sessions focus on simple, practical, evidence-informed techniques that can help participants feel more grounded, calmer, and better able to cope with challenging situations.

What You Will Learn

The workshops introduce key ideas about stress and anxiety, including common physical symptoms, emotional reactions, thought patterns, and behavioural responses.

Participants will also learn useful tools such as breathing exercises, relaxation methods, grounding techniques, mindfulness-based practices, and healthier coping strategies for daily life.

Workshop Topics

Understanding stress and anxiety

Recognising physical, emotional, and mental signs

Relaxation and breathing techniques

Mindfulness and grounding exercises

Building healthier coping strategies
